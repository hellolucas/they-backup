As many as 1 in 8 employees in the Netherlands suffer from stress. But the signs of stress are often neglected. For the Dutch Ministry of Social Affairs, THEY developed a social media platform that helps acknowledge, recognize and diminish work induced stress symptoms: "Herken de druppel" (Recognize the Drop).
The platform was developed in collaboration with BKB. Take a look here:  "Herken de druppel" (Recognize the Drop), and test yourself: "Check je Werkstress".
